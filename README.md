# SHIFTING SHADOWS

> A stealth horror game where the monster learns from every soul you collect.

You were once the kingdom's greatest knight, feared as **The Silver Shadow**. Every life you took has returned as a phantom, and now your past refuses to stay buried.

Built for **Mini Jam 215: Phantoms**, SHIFTING SHADOWS is a stealth horror game made entirely with **HTML5 Canvas** and **JavaScript**.

---

## Features

- 📖 Three story chapters with an evolving narrative
- 👻 Adaptive Phantom AI that grows stronger throughout the game
- 🗺️ Six handcrafted levels introducing new phantom abilities
- 🔥 Endless Hell Mode with six unique phases
- 💀 Secret Nightmare Mode unlocked after Chapter 3
- 🧲 Physics-based item carrying and throwing
- 🧠 Sanity and Corruption systems
- 🎵 Dynamic audio generated using WebAudio

---

## Gameplay

Your goal is to collect **Soul Essences** scattered throughout each level.

- Carry one Soul Essence at a time.
- Deliver it to the Rift.
- Every delivery causes the Rift to relocate.
- Once enough souls have been delivered, the Rift becomes active.
- Escape through the glowing Rift to complete the level.

As you progress, the Phantom becomes increasingly dangerous. It begins nearly harmless but gradually learns to:

- Detect sound
- Hunt the player
- See movement
- Phase through walls
- Split into multiple phantoms

Every Soul Essence collected makes survival more difficult.
